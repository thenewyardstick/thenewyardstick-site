TNY 4 Jul 2026


SEAT Ibiza
Deal price: £16,335
Saving: Approx. £5,275

While the current SEAT Ibiza is beginning to show its age, that only strengthens its appeal as a pre-registered bargain. Powered by a 94hp 1.0-litre turbocharged petrol engine, it reaches 62mph in around 11 seconds while returning around 55mpg. The Ibiza continues to offer sharp styling, a well-proven platform and a driving experience that remains competitive in the supermini class. Often overshadowed by newer rivals, it deserves to feature on a buyer's shortlist, especially at this price. (Image Credit: SEAT Press)
