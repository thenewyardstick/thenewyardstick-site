Chery Guide

TNY 17 Jul 2026

Chery is one of China's largest car manufacturers, yet only recently entered the UK market under its own name. Alongside sister brands Omoda and Jaecoo, Chery focuses on spacious, well-equipped SUVs that combine competitive pricing with generous standard equipment. Most models are offered with either petrol or plug-in hybrid power, giving buyers plenty of choice while undercutting many established rivals on price.

Current Models

Chery Tiggo 4 (26-)

RRP From : £19,995
Pre Reg From : c. £19,000
Used Price From : c. £19,000
Powertrains : Hybrid
Dimensions : 4320mm × 1831mm × 1653mm
Boot : 430L
Key rivals : Dacia Duster, Hyundai Kona, MG ZS Hybrid+, Suzuki S-Cross, Vauxhall Frontera 

The Tiggo 4 offers one of the cheapest routes into a family-sized SUV, combining generous equipment with a roomy cabin and keen pricing. It isn't the most polished option in its class, but it represents excellent value for buyers who prioritise space, equipment and affordability.

Chery Tiggo 7 (25-)

RRP From : £24,995
Pre Reg From : c. £20,000
Used Price From : c. £20,000
Powertrains : Petrol, PHEV
Dimensions : 4553mm × 1862mm × 1686mm
Boot : 565L (426L PHEV)
Key rivals : Kia Sportage, MG HS, Nissan Qashqai, Peugeot 3008, Volkswagen Tiguan

The Tiggo 7 sits in the heart of the family SUV market, taking on favourites such as the Kia Sportage and Nissan Qashqai. Strong equipment levels, a spacious interior and an efficient plug-in hybrid make it a compelling value choice, although some rivals still offer a more polished driving experience.

Chery Tiggo 8 (25-)

RRP From : £28,545
Pre Reg From : c. £24,500
Used Price From : c. £23,500
Powertrains : Petrol, PHEV
Dimensions : 4725mm × 1860mm × 1705mm
Boot : 694L (117L 7-seat)
Key rivals : Hyundai Santa Fe, Kia Sorento, Peugeot 5008, Skoda Kodiaq, Volkswagen Tayron

With seven seats, a generous boot and family-friendly practicality, the Tiggo 8 punches above its price point. It offers much of the space of more expensive rivals while keeping costs low, making it a tempting alternative for larger families who don't want to stretch to premium brands

Chery Tiggo 9 (25-)

RRP From : £38,545
Pre Reg From : c. £38,500
Used Price From : c. £34,500
Powertrains : PHEV
Dimensions : 4810mm × 1925mm × 1741mm
Boot : 819L (143L 7-seat)
Key rivals : Hyundai Santa Fe PHEV, Kia Sorento PHEV, Mazda CX-80, Peugeot 5008 PHEV, Volvo XC90

The Tiggo 9 is Chery's flagship SUV, offering genuine seven-seat practicality, a high-quality interior and a powerful plug-in hybrid powertrain. It delivers impressive value against much more expensive large SUVs.
