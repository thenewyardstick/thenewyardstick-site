MG: The UK’s Value Champion — Six MGs That Prove It

📈 Market Watch 4 Jul 2026

Across the UK, brand‑new and pre‑reg bargains are increasingly dominated by Stellantis, Renault Group, and a growing wave of new Chinese brands offering aggressive pricing across petrol, hybrid, and EV segments. But when you look at the deals that genuinely move the needle — strong equipment, sensible running costs, and real‑world value - one brand consistently comes out on top: MG.

In this Market Watch breakdown, we highlight the six best‑value MGs on sale today, proving why MG currently owns the value crown.

MG3
Deal price: £14,128
Saving: Approx. £3,367

The MG3 1.5 remains one of the strongest value propositions in the UK small‑car market. With a saving of over £3,300 against its £17,495 RRP, it delivers genuine bargain territory while still offering the space, equipment and everyday usability buyers expect from a modern supermini.

The 1.5‑litre petrol engine keeps running costs sensible, and MG’s recent improvements in cabin quality make the 3 feel more mature than its price suggests. For drivers seeking maximum value in a brand‑new or pre‑reg deal, the MG3 continues to punch well above its weight. (Image Credit: MG Press)


MG4 XPOWER
Deal price: £22,990
Saving: Approx. £11,005

The MG4 XPower is one of the most astonishing value propositions currently available in the UK performance EV space. With a saving of just over £11,000 against its £33,995 RRP, it delivers genuine hot‑hatch pace for supermini money.

Dual‑motor all‑wheel drive, 435hp, and sub‑4‑second acceleration put it in territory usually reserved for cars costing twice as much, yet it remains practical, well‑equipped, and surprisingly refined. For buyers seeking maximum performance per pound, the XPower is almost impossible to ignore. (Image Credit: MG Press)


MG HS PHEV (24.7KWh)
Deal price: £22,992
Saving: Approx. £9,503

The MG HS PHEV delivers one of the strongest value propositions in the UK plug‑in market. With a saving of just over £9,500 against its £32,495 RRP, it offers family‑SUV space, a generous equipment list and a smooth plug‑in hybrid powertrain for the kind of money many brands charge for a basic hatchback.

The real kicker is that the non‑hybrid HS starts from just £17,999, making the entire HS range one of Britain’s most affordable routes into a genuinely spacious crossover. For buyers seeking maximum value in both brand‑new and pre‑reg deals, the HS PHEV stands out as a top‑tier bargain. (Image Credit: MG Press)


MG IM5 75kWh
Deal price: £29,000
Saving: Approx. £10,950

The MG IM5 75kWh delivers premium EV dimensions for a fraction of the usual segment cost. With a saving of nearly £11,000 against its £39,950 RRP, it offers the footprint and cabin space of a Mercedes EQE saloon, yet at less than half the price of an equivalent EQE 300 model.


MG IM6 100kWh
Deal price: £32,990
Saving: Approx. £15,505

The MG IM6 100kWh takes MG’s value story into genuinely large‑EV territory. With 402hp, a 388‑mile range, and a huge 665‑litre boot, it offers the kind of space and performance normally associated with far more expensive models.

Crucially, the IM6 itself is larger than a Tesla Model Y, giving families generous cabin room and road presence while still undercutting most rivals by thousands. With a saving of over £15,500 against its £48,495 RRP, the IM6 stands out as one of the most compelling big‑EV deals currently available in the UK. (Image Credit: MG Press)


MG Cyberster GT
Deal price: £46,995
Saving: Approx. £13,250

The MG Cyberster GT is the clearest proof that MG now owns the value conversation in Britain. With a saving of around £13,250 against its £60,245 RRP, it delivers 496hp, 725Nm, and a 0–62 time of 3.2 seconds - numbers that push it firmly into supercar‑baiting territory.

The dual‑motor setup gives it huge straight‑line pace, while the dramatic, showcar‑inspired design makes it one of the most striking EVs on the road. As a flagship, it captures MG’s formula perfectly: bold styling, serious performance and unbeatable value in both brand‑new and pre‑reg deals. (Image Credit: MG Press)
