Ford Guide

TNY 17 Jul 2026

Ford has changed dramatically in recent years, saying goodbye to iconic models such as the Fiesta and Focus as it reshapes its model range. While that strategy has attracted mixed reactions, today's line-up still offers plenty to recommend. The Puma and Tourneo Courier are among the strongest value-focused models in their respective classes, while the Mustang and Mustang Mach-E remind buyers that Ford can still build exciting, aspirational cars alongside everyday family transport. Whether you're looking for practicality, performance or value, there's likely a Ford to suit.

Current Models

Ford Puma (19-)

RRP From : £26,245
Pre Reg From : c. £19,500
Used Price From : c. £7,500

Powertrains : MHEV, EV
Dimensions : 4214mm x 1805mm x 1555mm
Boot : 456L , 523L (EV)

Key rivals : Kia Stonic, Nissan Juke, Renault Captur, Toyota Yaris Cross, Vauxhall Mokka

The Puma remains one of Britain's best-value compact SUVs thanks to sharp pricing, clever practicality and an impressively large boot. Efficient mild-hybrid engines, a new electric version and comfortable everyday manners ensure it continues to appeal to a wide range of buyers.

Ford Tourneo Courier (24-)

RRP From : £28,490
Pre Reg From : £28,490
Used Price From : c. £20,500

Powertrains : Petrol, Diesel, EV
Dimensions : 4337mm x 1800mm x 1817mm
Boot : 570L

Key rivals : Citroën Berlingo, Dacia Jogger, Peugeot Rifter, Renault Kangoo

The Tourneo Courier is one of Ford's standout models, offering a rare blend of affordability, desirability and exceptional practicality. Sliding rear doors, low running costs and an impressively spacious cabin make it a genuine family-car alternative. If you're disappointed the Focus has gone, don't overlook this - it may be the smartest everyday Ford currently on sale.

Ford Kuga (20-)

RRP From : £33,335
Pre Reg From : c. £25,000
Used Price From : c. £8,000

Powertrains : Petrol, MHEV, PHEV
Dimensions : 4615mm x 1882mm x 1678mm
Boot : 536L, 412L (PHEV)

Key rivals : Hyundai Tucson, Kia Sportage, Nissan Qashqai, Vauxhall Grandland

The Kuga offers generous passenger space, competitive pricing and an excellent range of petrol and hybrid powertrains. Comfortable ride quality, a modern interior and strong practicality make it one of the easiest family SUVs to recommend, balancing refinement, value and everyday usability exceptionally well.

Ford Explorer (24-)

RRP From : £33,685
Pre Reg From : c. £23,500
Used Price From : c. £22,000

Powertrains : EV
Dimensions : 4468mm x 1871mm x 1639mm
Boot : 450L

Key rivals : Citroën E-C5, MG S5, Renault Scenic E-Tech, Volkswagen ID.4

Ford's Explorer gives buyers a well-rounded family EV with competitive range, strong technology and reassuring everyday usability. Built on Volkswagen ID.4 electric underpinnings, it delivers impressive refinement and value while remaining practical enough to suit family life without feeling unnecessarily large.

Ford Capri (24-)

RRP From : £35,485
Pre Reg From : c. £29,000
Used Price From : c. £23,000

Powertrains : EV
Dimensions : 4634mm x 1872mm x 1626mm
Boot : 572L

Key rivals : Kia EV6, Nissan Ariya, Peugeot E-408, Toyota C-HR+, Volkswagen ID.5

The Capri name may divide opinion, but the car itself offers generous passenger space, a large boot and impressive value, particularly as a pre-registered purchase. Comfortable, refined and practical, it succeeds as a family electric SUV regardless of the badge it wears.

Ford Mustang Mach-e (21-)

RRP From : £47,890
Pre Reg From : c. £29,000
Used Price From : c. £14,500

Powertrains : EV
Dimensions : 4713mm x 1881mm x 1624mm
Boot : 402L

Key rivals : BYD Sealion 7, DS No8, Hyundai Ioniq 5, Tesla Model Y, Volvo EX60

The Mustang Mach-E combines eye-catching styling with strong performance, competitive range and generous equipment. Comfortable enough for daily driving yet distinctive enough to stand apart from many rivals, its excellent pre-registered value makes it easy to see why many buyers would choose Ford's flagship EV over the Explorer or Capri.

Ford Mustang (24-)

RRP From : £57,670
Pre Reg From : c. £57,000
Used Price From : c. £46,000

Powertrains : Petrol
Dimensions : 4811mm x 1916mm x 1397mm
Boot : 382L

Key rivals : BMW M2, Lotus Emira, Toyota GR Supra

The latest Mustang remains one of the most characterful performance cars on sale, pairing its naturally aspirated V8 with a more modern interior and improved technology. Competitive pricing, everyday usability and unmistakable styling ensure it continues to offer outstanding value in an increasingly electrified market.

Ford Ranger (23-)

RRP From : £43,500
Pre Reg From : c. £34,000
Used Price From : c. £19,500

Powertrains : Diesel, PHEV
Dimensions : 5370mm x 1918mm x 1884mm
Boot : Pick-up bed

Key rivals : Isuzu D-Max, Mitsubishi L200, Toyota Hilux, Volkswagen Amarok

The Ranger is one of the Toyota Hilux's closest rivals, combining impressive towing ability with a comfortable, technology-rich cabin. Built alongside the Volkswagen Amarok, diesel and plug-in hybrid models make it equally capable as a workhorse or everyday family vehicle.

Ford Tourneo Connect (22-)

RRP From : £35,330
Pre Reg From : c. £30,500
Used Price From : c. £15,000

Powertrains : Petrol, Diesel, PHEV
Dimensions : 4505mm (4853mm LWB) x 1855 x 1833 mm 
Boot : 1213L, 1720L (LWB)

Key rivals : Citroën Berlingo, Mercedes-Benz Citan Tourer, Volkswagen Caddy Life

Sharing its underpinnings with the Volkswagen Caddy gives the Tourneo Connect a refined driving experience and a large spacious interior. Huge luggage capacity, seven-seat availability and efficient petrol, diesel and plug-in hybrid engines make it a hugely capable family MPV.

Ford Tourneo Custom (23-)

RRP From : £60,493
Pre Reg From : c. £37,500
Used Price From : c. £30,000

Powertrains : Diesel, PHEV, EV
Dimensions : 5050mm (5450mm LWB) x 2032mm x 1959mm 
Boot : 672L, 819L (LWB)

Key rivals : Citroën SpaceTourer, Peugeot Traveller, Volkswagen Multivan

The Tourneo Custom combines outstanding passenger space with strong refinement. Available with diesel, PHEV and EV power, it comfortably serves as a family MPV, executive shuttle or lifestyle vehicle. Its close relationship with the Volkswagen Multivan is also important, bringing shared engineering beneath different styling.
