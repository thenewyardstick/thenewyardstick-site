Audi Guide

TNY 15 Jul 2026

Audi has built its reputation on combining premium quality, advanced technology and understated design across one of the industry's broadest model ranges. From efficient hatchbacks to high-performance RS models and a rapidly expanding electric line-up, the brand offers something for almost every buyer. Strong refinement, high-quality interiors and competitive real-world pricing continue to make Audi one of the UK's most popular premium manufacturers, despite facing increasingly capable rivals from Germany, China and beyond.

Current Models

Audi A3 (20-)

RRP From : £30,895
Pre Reg From : c. £25,000
Used Price From : c. £9,000
Powertrains : Petrol - Diesel - MHEV - PHEV
Dimensions :  4352mm x 1816mm x 1441mm
Boot : 380L - 280L (PHEV)
Key rivals : BMW 1-Series, DS No4, Mercedes-Benz A-Class, Toyota Corolla

Based on the Volkswagen Golf, the Audi A3 combines premium quality with a technology-focused cabin and competitive lease deals. The standard petrol, diesel and mild-hybrid models offer the best balance, while the RS3 remains one of the few genuine rivals to the Mercedes-AMG A45.

Audi A5 (24-)

RRP From : £43,510
Pre Reg From : c. £35,000
Used Price From : c. £26,500
Powertrains : Petrol, Diesel, MHEV, PHEV
Dimensions :  4829mm x 1860mm x 1444mm
Boot : 445L - 476L (Avant)
Key rivals : BMW 3-Series, BYD Seal, Mercedes-Benz C-Class

Replacing the long-running A4, the latest A5 has grown in size to offer greater space, improved refinement and a more advanced cabin. Its versatile hatchback design adds everyday practicality, while the Avant provides even greater carrying capacity without sacrificing premium appeal.

Audi A6 (25-)

RRP From : £52,885
Pre Reg From : c. £37,000
Used Price From : c. £36,000
Powertrains : MHEV Petrol & Diesel, PHEV
Dimensions :  4999mm x 1875mm x 1472mm
Boot : 492L, 503L (Avant), 331L (PHEV) 
Key rivals : BMW 5-Series, Lexus ES, Mercedes E-Class

As Audi's larger executive model, the latest A6 combines generous space, advanced technology and electrified petrol and diesel engines. Available as a traditional saloon or Avant, it undercuts equivalent BMW 5 Series and Mercedes-Benz E-Class models while offering a similarly premium experience and impressive long-distance comfort.

Audi A6 E-Tron (24-)

RRP From : £63,365
Pre Reg From : c. £36,500
Used Price From : c. £35,000
Powertrains : EV
Dimensions :  4928mm x 1923mm x 1455mm
Boot : 502L 
Key rivals : BMW i5, DS No8, Mercedes EQE, Volvo ES90

The A6 e-tron showcases Audi's latest electric technology with a driving range of up to 464 miles and a refined, high-tech cabin. Generous dealer discounts help offset the depreciation affecting large electric saloons, allowing it to compete strongly with established rivals and emerging premium EVs from Volvo, DS and Chinese brands.

Audi Q3 (25-)

RRP From : £39,065
Pre Reg From : c. £35,000
Used Price From : c. £34,000
Powertrains : Petrol, Diesel, MHEV, PHEV
Dimensions :  4531mm x 1859mm x 1588mm
Boot : 488L, 375L (PHEV)
Key rivals : Alfa Romeo Tonale, BMW X1, Kia Sportage, Peugeot 3008, Volvo XC40

Competing in one of the market's most crowded SUV segments, the latest Q3 combines sharper styling, a more aerodynamic design and a technology-focused interior. The 2.0 TFSI and 2.0 TDI engines offer the best all-round performance, efficiency and refinement, helping the Q3 stand out from increasingly capable rivals.

 Audi Q4 E-Tron (21-)

RRP From : £46,955
Pre Reg From : c. £33,500
Used Price From : c. £13,000
Powertrains : EV
Dimensions :  4588mm x 1865mm x 1639mm
Boot : 520L 
Key rivals : Alpine A390, DS No7, Hyundai Ioniq 5, Nissan Ariya

Serving as the electric alternative to the Q3, the Q4 e-tron combines premium quality, sharp styling and a refined, comfortable driving experience. Strong depreciation has created attractive used-market prices, making it an appealing entry into premium electric motoring while remaining competitive against a growing list of rivals.

Audi Q5 (25-)

RRP From : £53,110
Pre Reg From : c. £ 44,000
Used Price From : c. £38,500
Powertrains : Petrol, Diesel, MHEV, PHEV
Dimensions :  4717mm x 1900mm x 1647mm
Boot : 520L, 438L (P HEV)
Key rivals : Alfa Romeo Stelvio, BMW X3, Mazda CX-60, Volvo XC60

The latest Q5 builds on Audi's global best-selling SUV with a bold new design, digital-first interior and a choice of petrol, diesel and plug-in hybrid powertrains. Entering an increasingly crowded market, it remains a refined, high-quality and thoroughly capable premium family SUV.

Audi Q6 E-Tron (24-)

RRP From : £61,315
Pre Reg From : c. £50,000
Used Price From : c. £39,000
Powertrains : EV
Dimensions :  4771mm x 1939mm x 1702mm
Boot : 526L 
Key rivals : BMW iX3, Lexus RZ, Peugeot E-5008, Tesla Model Y, Volvo EX60

The Q6 e-tron showcases Audi's latest electric SUV technology, combining a modern interior, premium refinement and up to 400 miles of range in long-range form. Performance remains a priority too, with the 490hp SQ6 delivering sports car pace while retaining the practicality expected of a family SUV.

Audi Q7 (15-)

RRP From : £73,225
Pre Reg From : c. £59,000
Used Price From : c. £7,000
Powertrains : Petrol. Diesel, PHEV
Dimensions :  5052mm x 1970mm x 1734mm
Boot : 291L (780L if 3rd row folded)
Key rivals : BMW X7, Land-Rover Discovery, Mercedes-Benz GLS, Volvo XC90

Audi's largest SUV continues to offer outstanding seven-seat practicality, refined long-distance comfort and exceptional value on the used market. An all-new Q7 is due shortly, bringing Audi's latest design language, more advanced technology and even greater space, ensuring it remains a key player in the premium SUV segment.

Audi Q8 (18-)

RRP From : £82,120
Pre Reg From : c. £57,000
Used Price From : c. £23,500
Powertrains : Petrol, Diesel, PHEV
Dimensions :  4986mm x 1995mm x 1678mm
Boot : 605L, 439L (PHEV) 
Key rivals : BMW X5/X6, Lexus RX, Maserati Grecale, Mercedes-Benz GLE

Audi's flagship SUV remains a compelling luxury SUV, with generous space, impressive refinement and effortless long-distance comfort. Significant discounts mean buyers can often enjoy BMW X6 levels of size, presence and equipment for little more than the price of a BMW X3, making the Q8 one of the premium market's standout value buys.

Audi E-Tron GT (21-)

RRP From : £89,555
Pre Reg From : c. £ 75,000
Used Price From : c. £25,500
Powertrains : EV
Dimensions :  4990mm x 1960mm x 1410mm
Boot : 405L, 350L (RS)
Key rivals : BMW i5, Lotus Emeya, Mercedes Benz EQE, Volvo ES90

Closely related to the Porsche Taycan, the e-tron GT takes a more performance-focused approach than the A6 e-tron. Sleek styling, exceptional refinement and immense acceleration place it among a select group of large electric saloons capable of delivering genuine supercar-rivalling pace.
