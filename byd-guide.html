BYD Guide

TNY 17 Jul 2026

BYD has rapidly become one of the UK's fastest-growing car brands, building its reputation on competitive pricing, advanced battery technology and generous standard equipment. What began as an electric-only manufacturer has quickly expanded into plug-in hybrids and family SUVs, giving buyers a wide choice of practical, value-focused models. From the compact Dolphin Surf and Atto 2 to the Seal saloon and spacious Sealion range, BYD now competes across some of Britain's most important market segments. With new models arriving at a remarkable pace, the brand looks set to become an increasingly familiar sight on UK roads.
